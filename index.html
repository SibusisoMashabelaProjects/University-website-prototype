OUR FACILITIES

The facilities of our institution are meant to impact the overall learning process as well as the mental and physical growth of our students.Our facilities are meant to positively affect the health, behavior, engagement, learning, and growth of our students. The physical and emotional health of students and teachers also depends on the facilities they are getting in our institute. So, our institution is equipped with proper facilities for the students and teachers, such that an environment conducive to learning is created.

Library

The university library also serves as a learning space for students to do independent work, use computers, access the internet, use equipment and research materials; to host special events such as author visits and book clubs; and for tutoring and testing.

Accomodation

The purpose of our accommodations is to provide students with equal access to learning and an equal opportunity to show what they know and what they can do.

Sports

We are proud of our reputation for sporting excellence, which spans performance, facilities, expertise and working partnerships, but one thing we are incredibly proud of is how broad our sports offer is. Whether you are a complete beginner looking to try something new or an elite athlete with a record of high achievement, our campus is home to some of the best sporting opportunities in the world.
